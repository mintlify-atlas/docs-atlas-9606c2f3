NextAudit AI is an intelligent SaaS audit platform built on a cyber-industrial microservices architecture. It transforms raw technical telemetry from distributed endpoint fleets into auditable, traceable decisions — giving IT, security, and operations teams the observability and governance they need to meet internal policies or regulatory requirements without manual effort.

## Vision

The platform exists to close the gap between infrastructure data and compliance evidence. Rather than requiring engineers to manually correlate logs, run ad-hoc queries, and produce reports, NextAudit AI continuously collects endpoint telemetry, applies AI-assisted analysis, and automates audit workflows — producing a governed, auditable record of your fleet's state at any point in time.

<Info>
  NextAudit AI is currently evolving toward a full SaaS product, with active development on the `develop` branch. The architecture documented here reflects the current microservices stack.
</Info>

## Key capabilities

NextAudit AI delivers three core areas of value:

**Fleet observability** — Real-time endpoint inventory, security posture, and vulnerability data powered by FleetDM and osquery. Every device in your fleet becomes a queryable, observable node.

**AI-assisted analysis** — Flowise provides AI agent flows that analyze fleet data, surface anomalies, and assist operators in understanding and responding to audit findings. Ollama runs LLM inference on-premises, keeping sensitive data private.

**Automated compliance** — n8n orchestrates audit workflows that run continuously against your fleet, enforcing policies and generating traceable output without manual intervention.

<Note>
  All services are deployed as Docker containers and coordinated through Docker Compose, enabling reproducible deployments across development, testing, and production environments.
</Note>
